Rebuilding the society

Burning Man is a network of people inspired by the values reflection in the Ten Princliples and united in the pursuit of a more creative and connected existence in the world. Throught the year we work to build Black Rock City, home of the largest annual Burning Man gathering, and nurture the distinsive culture emerging from that experience.
